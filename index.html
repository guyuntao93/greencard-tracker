说明Notes

  • 表A（Final Action Dates，最终裁定日）：优先日早于此日期时，移民签证/绿卡可获批准与签发。
  • 表B（Dates for Filing，递交申请日）：优先日早于此日期时，可提交 I-485 调整身份申请（需 USCIS 当月接受表B）。
  • 日期指“优先日截止线”。例如表A为 2023年6月1日，意味优先日在该日之前的申请人可被排到。
  • 数据覆盖 的月度签证公告（美国财年 FY2024 起）。
  • 数据来源：美国国务院签证公告 travel.state.gov。本页为信息参考，非法律建议。
  • Chart A (Final Action Dates): when your priority date is earlier than this, an immigrant visa / green card can be approved and issued.
  • Chart B (Dates for Filing): when your priority date is earlier than this, you may file the I-485 adjustment of status (only in months USCIS accepts Chart B).
  • Each value is a priority-date cutoff. E.g. Chart A = June 1, 2023 means applicants with a priority date before that date are eligible.
  • Coverage: monthly bulletins (from U.S. fiscal year FY2024).
  • Source: U.S. Dept. of State Visa Bulletin, travel.state.gov. For information only; not legal advice.
